ALOO POORI

Aloo poori is a popular Indian dish consisting of deep-fried bread (poori) served with a spicy potato (aloo) curry. The poories are crispy and golden brown, while the aloo curry is flavorful with spices like cumin, turmeric, and garam masala.

ALOO PARATHA

Aloo paratha is a popular North Indian flatbread stuffed with spiced mashed potatoes. The dough is rolled out, filled with the potato mixture, and then cooked on a griddle with ghee or oil until golden and crispy. It's often served with yogurt, pickles, or chutney.

RAJMA CHAWAL

Rajma Chawal is a popular North Indian dish consisting of red kidney beans cooked in a spiced tomato-onion gravy, served with steamed basmati rice. It's a comforting, flavorful meal often enjoyed with a side of pickles or yogurt.

Non-Veg
CHICKEN SANDWICH

A chicken sandwich typically consists of a breaded or grilled chicken fillet served between two slices of bread or a bun. It often includes lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise for added flavor and texture. This popular sandwich offers a satisfying combination of crispy chicken and fresh accompaniments in a handheld meal.

CHICKEN TIKKA MASALA

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish featuring marinated pieces of grilled chicken (tikka) in a creamy, spiced tomato-based sauce. The chicken is typically marinated in yogurt and spices like cumin and garam masala before being cooked and then simmered in a rich, tangy sauce flavored with garlic, ginger, and fenugreek.

CHILLI CHICKEN

Chilli Chicken is a popular Indo-Chinese dish featuring crispy fried chicken pieces tossed in a spicy, tangy sauce. It's flavored with garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and green chilies, offering a perfect balance of heat and savory flavors. Garnished with spring onions, it's a delicious appetizer or main course.

TANDOORI CHICKEN

Tandoori chicken is a popular Indian dish made by marinating chicken in a blend of yogurt and spices, including cayenne pepper, turmeric, and garam masala. The marinated chicken is traditionally cooked in a tandoor, a clay oven, resulting in tender, smoky, and flavorful meat with a vibrant red-orange hue.
